Ingredient

  • 1 small piece of cabbage
  • 2 tablespoon of yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey

Clean the cabbage with water. Blend cabbage, yogurt, and honey together. Apply to clean face for 20 minutes.

Ingredient

  • half a piece of Aloe
  • 1 egg (egg white only)

Peel off the Aloe skin, then blend aloe jell and egg white together. Apply to clean face for 15 minutes. This mask can brighten up the freckles and give your face feeling smooth after repeat every two weeks.
