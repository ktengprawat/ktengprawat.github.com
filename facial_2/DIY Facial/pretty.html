DIY Facial Mask

Why do facial?

Facial Mask is another good way to clean up and take care of your skin. It is like a spa treatment for your face. Natural ingredients in facial mask can help remove dead cell and heal damged skin. They are super easy to make, start today to helps you firm, clean, moisture, and brighten your skin! Pick the mask type on the left menu.

Anti Aging Mask

It's hard to keep a young looking skin. But, with these recipes, they will help you. It will prevent fine lines, wrinkles and firming your skin with these all natural ingredients that you can find in every supermarkets.

Green Apple + Lime

Both ingredients have high vitamin C which help anti aging and brightening skin.

Honey + Yogurt + Berries

Honey can speed healing and prevent infection. Yogurt help shrink the pores and mix berries and lemon juice have high vitamin C which help prevent aging skin.

Honey + Avocado

Honey keeps skin moisturize and soft. Avocado fat is rich in antioxidants and vitamin E which help firming the skin.

Anti Aging Recipe #1

  • 1 Whole Green Apple
  • 1 Teaspoon of Lime

Rinse and chop green apple. Blend the apple until smooth and add lime juice. Mix together and apply to clean face for 10 minutes.

Anti Aging Recipe #2

  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup of mix berries
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

In blender, combine ingredients until smooth. Apply on clean face, let mask sit for 10-15 min. then rinse face thoroughly with warm water and pat dry.

Anti Aging Recipe #3

  • 1/2 of a Ripe Avocado
  • 1/4 cup of Honey

Mash up your avocado and mix well with honey. Apply to face and neck generously, and take a breather for at least 20 minutes.

Brightening Masks

Got dark spots? No, worry! these masks got you covered! Do one of these mask at a time and repeat it weekly will bright up you skin and give you more even skin tone.

Cabbage + Honey + Yogurt

Cabbage help skin nourished with its vitamins A, B, C, K and P. Honey give skin moisturize and yogurt help shrink the pores.

Oats + Lime Juice

Oats is exfoliating and remove dead skin cells while lime juice is brighten and tighten skin and pores.

Honey + Egg + Milk

Honey and egg yolk are good for moisturize. Milk has a lot of good vitamins that can help keep the healthy skin and mineral help remove dead skin cell.

Brightening Recipe #1

  • 1 small piece of cabbage
  • 2 tablespoon of yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey

Clean the cabbage with water. Blend cabbage, yogurt, and honey together. Apply to clean face for 20 minutes.

Brightening Recipe #2

  • 2 tablespoon of cooked and mashed oats
  • 1 table spoon lime juice

Take 2 tablespoons of cooked and mashed oats in a bowl. Add a tablespoon of fresh lime juice in the bowl. Mix Well. Apply to clean face for 25 minutes or until it dry out.

Brightening Recipe #3

  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoon of fresh milk
  • 2 tablespoons of honey

Take the egg yolk out and place it in a bowl. Add milk and honey and mix them properly. Apply it to your face for at least 20 minutes.

Clean & Hydrate Masks

Wanna feel cleaner and more moisturize on you skin? Here is your the answer. After you apply one of these masks, it will help prevent your skin from damages and keep you face clean and hydrate.

Tomato + Lime Juice

Tomato can help protect against sunburn and skin ageing caused by sunlight exposure. Lime will help neutralize the oil production in your face.

Pumpkin + Honey

Pumpkin has high vitamin A, C and Zinc. Which heal the damaged skin and moisturizes to give you the clean and healthy glowing skin. Honey is good for moisturize skin and it can speed healing and prevent infection.

Papaya + Yogurt + Honey

Unripe papaya contains natural alpha-hydroxy acids and high levels of papain, an enzyme that helps dissolve dead skin cells. Yogurt brightens skin complexion and honey helps skin retain moisture.

Clean Mask Recipe #1

  • 1 half tomato
  • 2 tablespoon of lime juice

Mix the lime juice and the tomato in a blender or food processor. Process until the ingredients form a thin paste, approximately 15 to 20 seconds. With a clean brush or fingertips, apply a thin layer of the mixture to the skin. Leave for 15 minutes

Clean Mask Recipe #2

  • 2 teaspoons cooked or canned pumpkin, pureed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of honey

Mash the pumpkin and mix well with honey. Apply on face for 15 minutes.

Clean Mask Recipe #3

  • 1/2 cup unripe papaya, diced
  • 1 teaspoon plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon honey

Blend ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Apply to clean skin using fingers; leave on for 8 to 10 minutes.
