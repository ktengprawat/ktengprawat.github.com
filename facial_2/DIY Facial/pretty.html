DIY Facial Mask

Why do facial?

Facial Mask is another good way to clean up and take care of your skin. It is like a spa treatment for your face. Natural ingredients in facial mask can help remove dead cell and heal damged skin. Anti Aging Mask

It's hard to keep a young looking skin. But, with these recipes, they will help you. It will prevent fine lines, wrinkles and firming your skin with these all natural ingredients that you can find in every supermarkets.

Green Apple + Lime

Both ingredients have high vitamin C which help anti aging and brightening skin.

Honey + Yogurt + Berries

Honey can speed healing and prevent infection. Yogurt help shrink the pores and mix berries and lemon juice have high vitamin C which help prevent aging skin.

Honey + Avocado

Honey keeps skin moisturize and soft. Avocado fat is rich in antioxidants and vitamin E which help firming the skin.

Brightening Masks

Got dark spots? No, worry! these masks got you covered! Do one of these mask at a time and repeat it weekly will bright up you skin and give you more even skin tone.

Cabbage + Honey + Yogurt

Cabbage help skin nourished with its vitamins A, B, C, K and P. Honey give skin moisturize and yogurt help shrink the pores.

Oats + Lime Juice

Oats is exfoliating and remove dead skin cells while lime juice is brighten and tighten skin and pores.

Honey + Egg + Milk

Honey and egg yolk are good for moisturize. Milk has a lot of good vitamins that can help keep the healthy skin and mineral help remove dead skin cell.

Clean & Hydrate Masks

Wanna feel cleaner and more moisturize on you skin? Here is your the answer. After you apply one of these masks, it will help prevent your skin from damages and keep you face clean and hydrate.

Tomato + Lime Juice

Tomato can help protect against sunburn and skin ageing caused by sunlight exposure. Lime will help neutralize the oil production in your face.

Pumpkin + Honey

Pumpkin has high vitamin A, C and Zinc. Which heal the damaged skin and moisturizes to give you the clean and healthy glowing skin. Honey is good for moisturize skin and it can speed healing and prevent infection.

Papaya + Yogurt + Honey

Unripe papaya contains natural alpha-hydroxy acids and high levels of papain, an enzyme that helps dissolve dead skin cells. Yogurt brightens skin complexion and honey helps skin retain moisture.
