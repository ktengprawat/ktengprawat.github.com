Green tea Cupcakes

with green tea buttercream frosting

INGREDIENTS

GREEN TEA CUPCAKES:

  1. 2 1/2 cups cake flour, sifted
  2. 1 1/4 cups sugar
  3. 3 teaspoons baking powder
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 cup milk
  6. 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  7. 3 tablespoons matcha (green tea) powder
  8. 2 teaspoons almond extract
  9. 2 eggs

GREEN TEA BUTTERCREAM FROSTING

  1. 1 1/2 (6 ounces) unsalted butter, soften
  2. 2 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  3. 2 tablespoons milk
  4. 1 tablespoon green tea powder

DIRECTIONS

for cupcakes:

  1. 1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree F.
  2. 2. Mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  3. 3. Whisk together milk, oil and add to the dry ingredients. Beat 2 minutes on medium speed.
  4. 4. Add green tea powder, almond extract and eggs and beat again for 2 minutes on medium speed.
  5. 5. Pour batter into the pans, filling three-quarter of the way full. Bake about 15 minutes.
