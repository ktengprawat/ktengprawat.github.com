Green tea Cupcakes

with green tea buttercream frosting

INGREDIENTS

GREEN TEA CUPCAKES:

    2 1/2 cups cake flour, sifted

    1 1/4 cups sugar

    3 teaspoons baking powder

    1 teaspoon salt

    1 cup milk

    1/2 cup vegetable oil

    3 tablespoons matcha (green tea) powder

    2 teaspoons almond extract

    2 eggs

GREEN TEA BUTTERCREAM FROSTING

    1 1/2 (6 ounces) unsalted butter, soften

    2 1/2 cup powdered sugar

    2 tablespoons milk

    1 tablespoon green tea powder

DIRECTIONS

for cupcakes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree F.
  2. Mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  3. Whisk together milk, oil and add to the dry ingredients. Beat 2 minutes on medium speed.
  4. Add green tea powder, almond extract and eggs and beat again for 2 minutes on medium speed.
  5. Pour batter into the pans, filling three-quarter of the way full. Bake about 15 minutes.

for buttercream frosting

  1. Whisk the butter, add one-third of sugar and blend well.
  2. Add the remaining sugar one-third at a time, blend all together then add milk and green tea powder. Beat until light and fluffy.

Recipe by Andrea Maue, Ava James Catering, Cupcake Wars, Season 4
