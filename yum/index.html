Green tea Cupcakes

with green tea buttercream frosting

INGREDIENTS

GREEN TEA CUPCAKES:

    2 1/2 cups cake flour, sifted 1 1/4 cups sugar 3 teaspoons baking powder 1 teaspoon salt 1 cup milk 1/2 cup vegetable oil 3 tablespoons matcha (green tea) powder 2 teaspoons almond extract 2 eggs

GREEN TEA BUTTERCREAM FROSTING

  1. 1 1/2 (6 ounces) unsalted butter, soften
  2. 2 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  3. 2 tablespoons milk
  4. 1 tablespoon green tea powder

DIRECTIONS

for cupcakes:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree F.
  2. Mix together the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt
  3. Whisk together milk, oil and add to the dry ingredients. Beat 2 minutes on medium speed.
  4. Add green tea powder, almond extract and eggs and beat again for 2 minutes on medium speed.
  5. Pour batter into the pans, filling three-quarter of the way full. Bake about 15 minutes.
